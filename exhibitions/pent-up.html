PENT UP 2024
James Oliver Gallery 723 Chestnut St, 2nd Fl Philadelphia, PA Curated by Kylie Alies

PENT UP was a small group show of Philadelphia and New York City based artists curated by Kylie Alies. Tension turns into catharsis across the works in the exhibition, the stress of city living and contemporary anxiety becoming a rallying cry through moments of destruction or reassembly.
