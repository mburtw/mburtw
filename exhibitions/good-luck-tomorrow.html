← Exhibitions Good Luck Tomorrow 2025

Flow Space Gallery
526 W 26th St, #809
New York City, New York

'Good Luck Tomorrow' can be a gesture of goodwill or concern depending on one's inflection. Similarly, the paintings in this exhibition operate with ambiguous forces as their tone and affect flip depending on the constellation of images stitched together and the speed of the painted gesture.

Works included Everyone Loves A Sunset, 2025, 60 x 48", oil and printed silk on canvas Today Tomorrow and Yesterday, 2025, 26 x 36", oil and silk with digital print Good Luck Tomorrow, 2025, 46 x 48", oil with printed silk Rumor Mill, 2025, 26 x 46", oil on canvas with printed silk Circling the Flame, 2025, 26 x 36", oil on canvas with printed silk Over and Out, 2024, 18 x 22", asphalt, automotive glass, printed silk on panel
