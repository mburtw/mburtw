Good Luck Tomorrow 2025
Flow Space Gallery 526 W 26th St, #809 New York City, New York

'Good Luck Tomorrow' can be a gesture of goodwill or concern depending on one's inflection. Similarly, the paintings in this exhibition operate with ambiguous forces as their tone and affect flip depending on the constellation of images stitched together and the speed of the painted gesture.
