To Trace A Sunbeam Temple Contemportary at Tyler School of Art 2001 N 13th St, Philadelphia, PA<
The Schick Gallery at Skidmore College, 815 N Broadway, Saratoga Springs, NY
'To Trace A Sunbeam' is a site-specific, four to six channel, sculptural, video installation. Through her ongoing research, the artist proposes the car mirror as a direct relative to the claude glass, and composes the light installation out of hundreds of automotive mirrors salvaged from scrap yards and street corners. The broken mirrors disrupt the singular pespective of the source image, instead reflecting it into an immersive composition of ever-changing perspectives breaking with the canon of the genre.
