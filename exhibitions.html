Good Luck Tomorrow Flow Space Gallery
526 W 26th St, #809
New York City, New York
'Good Luck Tomorrow' can be a gesture of goodwill or concern depending on one's inflection. Similarly, the paintings in this exhibition operate with ambiguous forces as their tone and affect flip depending on the constellation of images stitched together and the speed of the painted gesture.

To Trace A Sunbeam Temple Contemporary, Tyler School of Art
2001 N 13th St, Philadelphia, PA

Schick Gallery, Skidmore College
815 North Broadway, Saratoga Springs, NY
To Trace A Sunbeam is a sculptural video installation that proposes the claude glass as relative to the car mirror. Through materials salvaged from Philadelphia's scrap yards and streets, projection is used as an intervention on the rubble to produce a monumentally scaled light installation.

PENT UP James Oliver Gallery
723 Chestnut St, 2nd Fl
Philadelphia, PA
PENT UP was a small group show of Philadelphia and New York City based artists curated by Kylie Alies. Tension turns into catharsis across the works in the exhibition, the stress of city living and contemporary anxiety becoming a rallying cry through moments of destruction or reassembly.
